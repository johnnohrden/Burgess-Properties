About Burgess Properties

Burgess Properties, LLC is a full-service commercial real estate brokerage firm specializing in the Boston and Suburban Boston markets.

Through a combination of product and tenant representation, Burgess Properties brings an ability to negotiate sale and lease transactions from both sides of the ownership table. Our clients and customers are the beneficiaries of years of experience, detailed market insight, and close relationships at both the local and institutional level. 

Unlike other brokerage firms whose members often bring little more than matchmaking experience to bear upon a deal, Burgess Properties offers extensive firsthand experience in every aspect of commercial real estate.

We encourage you to contact us whether you are a software company looking to relocate from California or a heavy manufacturer looking for a location to allow your use. Our goal is to offer you the highest level of service, integrity and professionalism and to have you call upon us again and again as your company's needs grow and change.

An early adoption and mastery of the technological side of the evolving brokerage business has resulted in Burgess Properties being regularly ranked by the Boston Business Journal as one of Boston’s top 20 Commercial Real Estate brokerage firms in terms of total square footage leased or sold, year after year.
